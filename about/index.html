About the Club

The CUHS Tech Club was established in 2013 for students interested in technology. All students are encouraged to participate in the various club activities.

The club holds monthly meetings on the 3rd or 4th Tuesday of the month at lunch. Game nights are held quarterly for all CUHS students to stay after school and play video games, board games and hang out with friends.

Tech club members participate in fundraisers throughout the year ranging from selling snow cones and hot chocolate at the football games to selling healthy snacks at school. These funds are used to pay for annual trip to a college campus in the spring.

Other trips we take during the year include a tour of Bitwise Industries in Fresno in November and the annual Hour of Code outreach at Fairmead and Dairyland schools each December.
